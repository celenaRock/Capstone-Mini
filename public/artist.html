ABOUT THE ARTIST: Jacqueline Rock

Jacqueline is a local artist in New Iberia, Louisiana. She is 9 years old and especially fond of unicorns.

"The pony I want to accessorize is bored of her life on a ship in the ocean. She decided to fly away to a nearby island. This island had fabulous dresses and accessories. There is an island princess who hosts dress-up contests. My unicorn always wins."
